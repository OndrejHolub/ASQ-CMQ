# 📘 ASQ CMQ/OE Preparation Platform

Welcome to the **ASQ CMQ/OE Preparation Platform** — a comprehensive browser‑based study resource designed to help learners master the *Certified Manager of Quality / Organizational Excellence* (CMQ/OE) Body of Knowledge.([asq.org](https://www.asq.org/cert/manager-of-quality?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

This site provides structured content, practice questions, flashcards, and deep topic summaries so you can study effectively chapter by chapter — all without leaving your browser.

---

## 🚀 What This Is

The **ASQ CMQ/OE Preparation Platform** is:

- 📖 **Study‑centric** – Detailed notes on quality management topics, aligned with key CMQ/OE body of knowledge concepts.([asq.org](https://www.asq.org/cert/manager-of-quality?utm_source=chatgpt.com))  
- 🧠 **Interactive** – Built‑in *flashcards* to drill concepts for better retention.  
- ❓ **Practice‑focused** – Quick quizzes to test your understanding chapter by chapter.  
- 📚 **Free & Accessible** – Runs in any modern web browser — no installation required.

Whether you’re preparing for the exam or simply brushing up on quality management and organizational excellence fundamentals, this platform is a useful companion.

---

## 📌 Features

✨ **Chapters** — Explore structured segments covering major certification topics (leadership, strategic planning, teams, process improvement, etc.).([ondrejholub.github.io](https://ondrejholub.github.io/ASQ-CMQ/))

🔗 **Cross‑referenced Material** — Link ideas to real‑world quality management practices.
